Happiness Class

林夕的词 原来我非不快乐，只我一人未发觉。

The first reason for having positive psychology

Question make a difference,which is why it is also important to ask the positive psychology question,which is the salutogenic question:”What is source of health? What is the source of success? What is the source of wellbeing?”

有的时候是我们所提的问题不对，比如我记得上初中的时候我的成绩在年纪前二十，可是我总是不开心，常常问自己到底是哪里不对，才让我成绩不够拔尖？后来上了高中，我的成绩一落千丈，我也问自己，怎么回事？现在想来，为什么不问自己，上初中的时候，为什么成绩会好呢?成绩比我更好的同学为什么成绩更好？一如后来为什么不问自己，会使我感到快乐的事是什么？难道我一直都是抑郁从来没有快乐？那些快乐的种子没喝过水，没沐浴过阳光，后来便枯萎了。

What Marva Collins did was help her students shift from the passive victim –you are victim of your circumstances,of your upbringing, neighborhood, country, whatever it is–from a passive victim to an active agent.
Yes,it’s difficult.It’s tough.It’s unfair.However,it’s your responsibility.No one is coming.It’s up to you to make that difference in your life.

The second reason

Getting rid of the negative dose not guarantee us the positive.
“Well, Let’s just get rid of that depression and everthing will be fine.” It won’t.

How can we get beyond that “quiet depression”? To excitement, to joy, to happiness?In order to do that,we need to cultivate these traits.Once again,they don’t spontaneously emerge once the painful experiences go away.

A每周工作80小时，他习惯逃避 逃避家事，逃避处理私事，这是疾病模型，让我们逃避不如意的事。B每周工作80小时，对工作充满热情，这是健康模型，让我们追求激情，追求喜欢做的事。对疾病模型为言，最优水平是零Let’s just be OK;Let’s just not hurt.that’s not enough.Let’s go to the excitement,to the fun.Because the ideal is not just a tensionless state. It is the creative tension.when we do “flow”.flow is the state where we are excited,where we are engaging what we are doing事实上，在那种状态下会有点不舒适，那是我们舒适区之外的，It’s being in our strech zone–not the panic zone,where it hurts –the stretch zone,其中有兴奋，还有点紧张，还可能会有成长
